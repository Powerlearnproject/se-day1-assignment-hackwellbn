Our Story

Rooted in craft,
poured with intention.

Ember & Roast began as a simple idea: that good coffee deserves the same care and attention as any fine craft. We source our beans directly from small farms across Ethiopia, Colombia, and Guatemala — building relationships that go far beyond the transaction.

Every roast is dialed in by hand in our back-room micro-roastery. Every cup is brewed to order. We don't rush the process, and we don't think you should either.
